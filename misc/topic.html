EFFECT OF CYBERSECURITY ON SOCIETY AND INDIVIDUALS

Cybersecurity is the art of protecting computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from unauthorised access or criminal use and the practice of criminal use and the practice of ensuring confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information. It is basically protecting every part of the computer and the internet from communication, entertainment, transportation, shopping, medicine, and more. As we rely on technology and care about our personal information stored in computers, smartphones, and tablets, cybersecurity needs to be everywhere to protect our information from the risks.
